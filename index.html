About the Sale

Odyssey Middle School is hosting a Native Plant Sale to help promote environmentally friendly gardening and support student projects. All plants are native to our region and help wildlife thrive.

Why Choose Native Plants?

Help Pollinators

Native plants provide food and habitat for bees, butterflies, and birds.

Save Water

They are adapted to the local climate and require less watering.

Low Maintenance

Native plants thrive naturally without fertilizers or special care.

Example Plants

Oregon Grape

Evergreen shrub with yellow flowers and berries loved by birds.

Red Flowering Currant

Beautiful spring flowers that attract hummingbirds.

Yarrow

A hardy plant that attracts pollinators and blooms all summer.
